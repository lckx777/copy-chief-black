# Task: Email Sequence

> **Primary Expert:** Andre Chaperon
> **Supporting:** John Carlton (voice), Gary Halbert (subject lines), Dan Kennedy (close)

---

## Structure: Soap Opera Sequence (5-7 emails)

```
EMAIL 1: THE SCENE        - Drop into vivid situation, open loop
EMAIL 2: THE BACKSTORY    - How it happened, deepen investment
EMAIL 3: THE EPIPHANY     - The discovery / mechanism reveal
EMAIL 4: THE PROOF        - Testimonials, results, social proof
EMAIL 5: THE TRANSFORMATION - What life looks like after
EMAIL 6: THE CALL         - Product as natural conclusion
EMAIL 7: THE LAST CHANCE  - Urgency + close (Kennedy)
```

## Per-Email Template

```
SUBJECT: [Halbert -- curiosity + specificity, lowercase personal style]
OPENER: [Collier -- enter the conversation in their mind]
BODY: [Chaperon -- narrative arc, open loops, personal tone]
CLOSE: [Kennedy -- CTA or cliffhanger for next email]
PS: [Halbert -- strongest benefit or urgency]
```

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Each email ends with open loop or cliffhanger
- [ ] No product mention before email 3
- [ ] Subject lines feel personal (not marketing)
- [ ] 300-500 words per email
- [ ] 3:1 value-to-pitch ratio across sequence
- [ ] Final email closes ALL open loops
- [ ] Plain text format (no HTML templates)
- [ ] Each email standalone valuable but incomplete
