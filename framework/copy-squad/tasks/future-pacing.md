# Task: Future Pacing (Positive + Negative)

> **Primary Expert:** Andre Chaperon (emotional arcs)
> **Supporting:** Gary Halbert (vivid imagery), Frank Kern (results in advance)

---

## Process

### Step 1: Negative Future Pace (Without Solution)
- Timeline: 30 days / 6 months / 1 year / 5 years
- For each timeline:
  - What specifically gets WORSE: ___
  - Who notices: ___
  - What they say: "___"
  - Physical manifestation: ___

### Step 2: Positive Future Pace (With Solution)
- Timeline: 7 days / 30 days / 90 days / 1 year
- For each timeline:
  - First small win: ___
  - Moment of realization: ___
  - What others notice first: ___
  - Identity shift: "I am now ___"

### Step 3: Contrast Bridge
Write the pivot sentence that connects negative to positive:
"Instead of [worst negative], imagine [best positive]"

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Both positive AND negative paces written
- [ ] Specific timelines (not "someday")
- [ ] Third-party reactions in both
- [ ] Identity-level transformation in positive
- [ ] Sensory details in at least 3 timeframes
- [ ] Contrast bridge is specific, not generic
