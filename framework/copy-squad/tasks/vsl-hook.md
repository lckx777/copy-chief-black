# Task: VSL Hook (0-60 seconds)

> **Primary Expert:** Gary Halbert (hook), Eugene Schwartz (awareness calibration)
> **Supporting:** Clayton Makepeace (power lead), Todd Brown (mechanism tease)

---

## Structure

```
0-3s:  PATTERN INTERRUPT - Stop the scroll (Halbert tabloid energy)
3-10s: IDENTIFICATION    - "If you [symptom/situation]..." (Schwartz)
10-20s: CURIOSITY GAP   - Open the information gap (Sugarman seed)
20-40s: MECHANISM TEASE  - Hint at the NEW discovery (Brown)
40-60s: COMMITMENT       - "Keep watching because..." (transition to body)
```

## Hook Types by Awareness

| Awareness | Hook Approach |
|-----------|--------------|
| Problem Aware | "If you [symptom], doctors say [wrong thing]. The real reason is..." |
| Solution Aware | "Forget [common solution]. [Expert] discovered [mechanism]..." |
| Product Aware | "[Product] just released the results of [study/test]..." |
| Unaware | "[Character] was [doing something] when [unexpected thing]..." |

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Pattern interrupt in first 3 seconds
- [ ] Awareness level matched correctly
- [ ] Curiosity gap created (can only close by watching)
- [ ] Mechanism teased but NOT revealed
- [ ] Specificity present: name, number, or vivid detail
- [ ] No product mention in first 30 seconds (unless product aware)
- [ ] Retention hook at 40-60s mark
- [ ] Would stop YOUR scroll?
