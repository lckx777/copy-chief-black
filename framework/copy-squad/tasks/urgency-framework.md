## 4-Layer Deadline Stack (Kennedy)

| Layer | Type | Template | Reason-Why Template |
|-------|------|----------|-------------------|
| 1 | TIME | "This offer expires [specific date] at [time]" | "Because [logistic reason]" |
| 2 | QUANTITY | "Only [exact number] available" | "Because [production/capacity limit]" |
| 3 | BONUS | "[Bonus] disappears after [date]" | "Because [cost/licensing reason]" |
| 4 | PRICE | "Price increases to $[amount] after [date]" | "Because [business reason]" |

## Urgency by Awareness Level

| Awareness | Urgency Type | Angle |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Most Aware | Direct deal urgency | "Save 40% -- ends Friday" |
| Product Aware | Social proof urgency | "2,847 ordered this week. [X] left." |
| Solution Aware | Discovery urgency | "Before this gets taken down..." |
| Problem Aware | Consequence urgency | "Every day without [solution], [mechanism] worsens" |
| Unaware | Curiosity urgency | "This won't be online much longer" |

## Consequence Framework

```
IF THEY ACT NOW:
  → [Immediate result] within [timeframe]
  → [Medium result] within [timeframe]
  → [Long-term transformation]

IF THEY DO NOT ACT:
  → [Immediate consequence] continues
  → [Escalation] happens within [timeframe]
  → [Ultimate consequence] becomes reality
```

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Minimum 2 deadline layers active
- [ ] Every deadline has a specific reason-why
- [ ] Deadlines use exact dates and times (not "limited time")
- [ ] Consequences of inaction are specific and escalating
- [ ] Urgency restated minimum 3 times in close
- [ ] Urgency matches awareness level
- [ ] Reason-why sounds like confession, not marketing
- [ ] No fake urgency (countdown timers that reset = trust death)
