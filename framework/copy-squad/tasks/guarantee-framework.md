## Guarantee Hierarchy

| Level | Type | Template | Power |
|-------|------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Standard | "Full refund if not satisfied within [days]" | WEAK |
| 2 | Enhanced | "Full refund, no questions asked, keep the bonuses" | MODERATE |
| 3 | Conditional | "[Specific result] in [timeframe] or full refund + $[bonus]" | STRONG |
| 4 | Nuclear | "[Specific result] in [timeframe] or I pay you $[amount] from my pocket" | NUCLEAR |

## Guarantee Construction Template

```
NAME: "[Name] Guarantee" (e.g., "Triple Protection Guarantee")

PROMISE: "If you don't [specific measurable result] within [timeframe]..."

REVERSAL: "...I'll [refund action] AND [extra compensation]."

REASON: "I can offer this because [specific statistic]% of people who
         follow the [method name] see results within [timeframe]."

PLACEMENT: Adjacent to CTA button. Visible. Not buried.
```

## Guarantee Naming Patterns

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Number + Protection | "Triple Protection Guarantee" |
| Timeframe + Promise | "90-Day Results Guarantee" |
| Outcome + Or Else | "Silence Your Ears or Pay Nothing" |
| Confidence + Name | "Iron-Clad Promise" |

## Reason-Why Templates

| Reason Type | Template |
|-------------|----------|
| Statistical | "94.7% of people who follow [method] see [result] within [time]" |
| Business | "An unhappy customer costs me more in refunds and support than..." |
| Confidence | "After [X years/users], I know this works for [specific type]" |
| Logical | "If it didn't work, I'd be out of business by now" |

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Guarantee is NAMED (not generic "satisfaction guaranteed")
- [ ] Tied to MEASURABLE outcome (not feeling)
- [ ] Specific TIMEFRAME stated
- [ ] Has reason-why for the guarantee
- [ ] Placed NEXT to the CTA
- [ ] At minimum Level 2 strength
- [ ] Risk feels entirely on the SELLER, not the buyer
- [ ] Language is plain and direct (Carlton -- no legalese)
